Kenyan Professional Photographer .

Karibu (Welcome) to my photofolio! I'm Israel Corby, a passionate photographer hailing from the breathtaking landscapes of Kenya. Growing up surrounded by the rich colors, vibrant culture, and diverse wildlife of this beautiful country, photography became not just a hobby, but a way for me to showcase the beauty of my surroundings to the world. My journey with photography began when I picked up my first camera during a safari trip with my family. Since then, I've been captivated by the power of images to tell stories and evoke emotions. My photography style is deeply influenced by the natural beauty of Kenya, from the golden savannas of the Maasai Mara to the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean. Whether I'm capturing the majesty of wildlife on safari or the vibrant street life of Nairobi, my aim is to convey the raw energy and authentic spirit of my homeland. With a background in visual storytelling and a passion for conservation, I strive to use my photography as a tool for raising awareness about environmental issues and celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Kenya. For me, photography is not just about taking pictures; it's about capturing moments that inspire, provoke thought, and spark change. Each photograph is a testament to the beauty of Kenya and the incredible stories waiting to be told. Asante sana (Thank you very much) for visiting my photofolio. I invite you to explore my portfolio and immerse yourself in the colors, textures, and stories of Kenya through my lens. I hope my photographs inspire you to see the world in a new light and ignite your own passion for exploration and discovery. Karibu tena (Welcome again)! Israel Corby
